Announcing Object Lateral

September 4, 2012

I’m excited to announce my first foray into business ownership!

Well, technically, my first time owning a business was when I bought 20 shares of KKD right before the Atkins Diet craze. Here’s hoping I do a little better this time around ;)

I spent six great years working on networks and writing software for RSDi, but the time has come for me to venture out and see what I can do on my own.

The company is called Object Lateral. We write thoughtful, custom software.

For now that will mostly be software for hire, but I do have a few product ideas that I might pursue in tandem.

The branding & design is by Cody Peterson, who is awesome (and available for contract).

Please take a moment to check out the site and let me know what you think!

We’re also on the Twitter, the GitHub, and even the FaceBook if you’re feeling social.

Oh yeah, and please do get in touch if you have software needs. I’d love to help in any way that I can!
